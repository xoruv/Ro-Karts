About Ro-Karts

Ro-Karts is an exhilarating anime-inspired racing game that combines high-speed action with strategic gameplay. Race through beautifully crafted tracks, master the art of drifting, and compete against players worldwide.

Drawing inspiration from the beloved Mario Kart series, Ro-Karts is designed to be kid-friendly while offering engaging gameplay that players of all ages will love! Experience the perfect blend of competitive racing and family-friendly fun.
